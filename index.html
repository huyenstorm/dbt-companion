The States of Mind

Our minds operate in three distinct states:

  • Reasonable Mind: Cool, rational, factual, and task-focused. It ignores emotional factors and operates on logic.
  • Emotion Mind: Hot, mood-driven, impulsive, and urgent. It distorts facts to match current feelings and drives reactiveness.
  • Wise Mind: The intuitive synthesis of both. It honors your emotions while checking the facts, identifying the middle path of wisdom.

Mindfulness 'What' Skills

Observe: Just notice physical sensations, thoughts, and emotions without label.
Describe: Put words on your experience. Label facts only (e.g. 'I feel my heart racing', not 'I'm dying').
Participate: Throw yourself fully into the current activity. Focus 100% on the now.
